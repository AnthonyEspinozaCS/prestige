Personal Care

At Prestige Home Healthcare, we firmly believe in the dignity and independence of every individual in our care. We acknowledge that personal support in the day-to-day tasks, such as bathing or dressing, can significantly enhance the quality of life, fostering contentment and happiness within the comfort of your own home.

Recognizing the unique requirements of each individual, we specialize in customizing personal care plans that reflect your needs. Our seasoned Care Professionals will diligently collaborate with you or your loved ones to craft a meticulous, person-centric care plan. This detailed blueprint ensures the provision of requisite care and support in a manner that best suits your preferences.

Our Personal Care encompasses:

  • An approach that upholds dignity and respect.
  • Maintenance of your desired routine and lifestyle.
  • Administration of care within the familiarity of your own home.
  • Assuring the family members of their loved one's well-being.
  • Offering respite to family members from their caregiving duties.
  • Enabling the individual to continue living at home.

We comprehend the unsettling nature of periods when daily living activities like personal hygiene, dressing, and personal grooming become a challenge. It might be particularly strenuous to accept help with personal care from our loved ones, especially when it involves intimate or clinical needs.

Your Care Professional from Prestige Home Healthcare, will carefully cater to your requirements, and will dedicate ample time to understand you to formulate a tailored care approach. They will strive to establish a trusted relationship to ensure the provision of personalized care, conducive to your independence.

Our Care Professionals receive extensive training to assist in personal care. This includes aiding in moving and positioning, medication support, mealtime assistance, bathing or showering, and continence care. We also provide assistance in more specialized personal care tasks, such as catheter and stoma care. Our goal is to facilitate you in leading a fulfilling life at home, fostering your best self.
