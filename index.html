Mission Statement

Prestige Home Healthcare is steadfastly committed to delivering unparalleled home care services that diligently focus on promoting the well-being and autonomy of our clientele. We take immense pride in our ability to offer compassionate and individualized care that is meticulously designed to accommodate the distinct needs and preferences of each client. Our primary objective is to enrich their quality of life significantly.

Our distinguishing hallmark lies in our unrelenting dedication to quality and excellence, a trait that differentiates us within the sector. As a trusted provider of home care services, we are honoured to serve individuals and families across the UK, further solidifying our reputation for commitment and reliability.

Geographic Overview

The UK has over 11,200 home care providers registered and regulated by their respective country's regulatory body, with England having the highest number of providers at 10,007.

Currently, more than 950,000 people receive domiciliary care in the UK, with England having the highest number of recipients at 814,000. Furthermore, the UK's domiciliary care workforce is substantial, with nearly 823,000 individuals employed in the industry.
