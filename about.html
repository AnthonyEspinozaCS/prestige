About Us

We are delighted to announce the esteemed contributions of Teresa Griffiths CBE and Dr. Gwenllian Hughes MBE to our organisation. Each brings a profound depth of experience and a considerable breadth of knowledge within the healthcare industry. Of particular note is their active involvement in healthcare-related projects and their contributions at the UK Government level, both of which significantly enhance the collective proficiency of our organisation.

Dr. Tim Tran, a licensed and highly reputable physician, is another key contributor to our team. He maintains an unwavering commitment to the ethical provision of patient care, consistently delivering services of an unparalleled standard. It is this unwavering devotion to honesty, dignity, and integrity that engenders profound trust from his patients and exemplifies the ethos of our organisation.

Leading the corporate facet of our board is Mr Dan Peña, the recipient of the Order of St. John, a distinction bestowed by the late Queen Elizabeth II. Mr Peña’s experience spans over five decades, during which he has demonstrated extraordinary prowess in concluding high-stakes, international deals. Accumulating over a trillion US dollars in equity through diverse global projects, he graciously commits his extensive expertise to our UK-based endeavour.

Our Finance Director, Francis Martin CBE, who formerly served as the Managing Partner of BDO NI and the immediate past President of the British Chambers of Commerce, lends his remarkable acumen to our team. Notably, he was the recipient of the "Deal Maker of the Year" award in 2016, an accolade that speaks volumes about his proficiency in concluding impactful transactions.

Furthermore, we are privileged to have Fiona Hobbs, our Legal Director hailing from the distinguished London firm, Linklaters. Fiona brings an impressive wealth of expertise in handling intricate legal matters, ensuring the successful conclusion of transactions. It is noteworthy to mention that both Francis and Fiona possess previous experience providing advisory services at the UK Government level, further strengthening our organisation's collective competency.

Team of Directors

Daniel S. Peña Sr.

Chairman of the Board

Daniel S. Peña, Sr. is a titan in business and finance, with a career spanning several decades and a proven track record of achieving quantum growth for his companies. He is the Founder and Chairman of The Guthrie Group (TGG), a globally renowned investment consortium specializing in facilitating transactions for companies and institutions. With offices in the UK and Asia, TGG has acted as principal, advisor, and agent to various regional and global entities...

Before founding TGG, Peña served as Chairman, President, and CEO of Great Western Resources Inc. (GWRI), a Houston-based natural resource company public on the London Stock Exchange (LSE). Under Peña's leadership, GWRI grew from just $820 to a massive $450 million in eight years, achieving an astounding growth rate of 542,000% during one of the worst energy market collapses in history. This remarkable achievement has made him a quantum growth strategist with a unique ability to create exponential growth even in the most challenging circumstances.

Before his time at GWRI, Peña co-founded JPK Industries, Inc., a vertically integrated petroleum company that grew to $50 million in three years. He also served as an investment banker with Bear Stearns and Co., advising clients in the US and internationally. Peña has negotiated successful transactions with multinationals, governments, and major international financial institutions, making him a sought-after advisor in business.

Peña's impressive credentials extend beyond the business world. He has served on the US Presidential Roundtable Senatorial Commission, the Executive Committee for The Alliance for a Safer LA, and as a financial advisor to entities such as the Vatican and the Los Angeles Police Protective League. He has also taught pro bono courses on entrepreneurship to minority business students at California State University, where he graduated with a BS in Business Administration in 1971.

In addition to his business and philanthropic endeavors, Peña is an accomplished sportsman, having received several awards for his achievements in golf and other athletic pursuits. He splits his time between Manila, Monte Carlo, and the UK, where he spends summers and holidays with his three grown children at their stunning 15th Century estate, Guthrie Castle, in Scotland. Peña and his children enjoy playing golf on their private course and working out in their fully equipped fitness facility.

Dr. Tim Tran

Founding Director

Dr. Tim Tran, esteemed within the medical fraternity, consistently prioritizes the delivery of exceptional healthcare to all patients under his care. With a comprehensive foundation in both medicine and surgery, Dr. Tran has remained unwavering in his commitment to furnish top-tier medical and surgical services spanning an array of specialties...

With a portfolio showcasing expertise in several surgical disciplines, including colorectal and vascular surgery, cardiothoracic surgery, and plastic surgery, Dr. Tran exhibits a remarkable breadth of knowledge. Furthermore, his proficiency extends to the realms of orthopaedics, general and emergency medicine, general practice, and intensive care. The encompassing depth of his competence is particularly evident in his adept management of critically ill patients and proficiency in Advanced Trauma and Life Support. His seasoned experience in both open and laparoscopic surgery solidifies his standing as a preeminent authority in clinical hospital and community care settings.

In the scope of his professional trajectory, Dr. Tran has held a significant position as a non-executive board member of the South Downs Federation. Currently, he fulfils the role of Managing Partner in a prosperous GP partnership in Sussex, UK. Dr. Tran holds an MBChB Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery from the University of Leicester, where he earned the honour of Distinction. His meritorious educational journey also includes admission as a member of the distinguished Royal College of Surgeons of England and the Royal College of General Practitioners.

Beyond his esteemed professional pursuits, Dr. Tran exhibits a fervour for running and cycling in his leisure time, with a particular emphasis on supporting philanthropic endeavors. He consistently contributes to the facilitation of medical projects through fund-raising efforts for esteemed organizations such as Thrombosis UK and the Marie Curie charity.

Dr. Tim Tran, with his expansive medical acumen and staunch commitment to delivering the highest level of patient care, is an invaluable contributor to the wider medical community.

Francis Martin

Finance Director

Francis Martin is a highly accomplished C-suite business leader, chartered accountant, corporate financier, and strategy advisor with 29 years of experience working with BDO Northern Ireland. He spent 24 years as a Partner, including two terms as a Managing Partner, where he served on the Leadership and Management Board of the BDO UK National Firm in London for six years...

In 2020, Francis joined Forster Chase as Managing Partner, a leading corporate finance, business advisory, and senior executive specialist firm based in London. Working with the Partners and Non-Executive Board, he leads on M&A and fundraising, including extensive experience in the M&A Healthcare sector.

Francis is an accomplished and senior business advisory professional with experience working at the highest levels of private sector business, from SMEs to multinationals across a broad spectrum of sectors representative of the UK economy. He has also worked with government business support agencies to provide board-level advice to clients, focusing on strategy, finance, mergers and acquisitions, capability building, governance and risk, growth, value creation, business transformation, and operational effectiveness.

In addition, Francis has held prestigious and high-profile non-executive business roles for the past 12 years, creating deep connections to business across the UK and internationally and access to and engagement with senior levels of government in Westminster and Northern Ireland. These roles include Board Member, President, and Chair of the British Chambers of Commerce, Board Member, President, and Chair of the Northern Ireland Chamber of Commerce, Member of the Agri-Food Strategy Board, and Board Member and Chair of Lagan College, Belfast, Northern Ireland's first and largest integrated, all-ability, coeducated secondary school.

In June 2019, he was awarded a CBE in the Queen's Birthday Honours List for Services to UK Business and Northern Ireland Dealmaker of the Year in 2016 by Inside Dealmaker Awards. With his extensive expertise, strategic vision, and exceptional leadership skills, Francis Martin is a valuable asset to the business community.

Fiona Hobbs

Legal Director

Fiona Hobbs is a highly accomplished solicitor and partner at Linklaters LLP, a top-tier global law firm with over 5,000 employees and 30 offices worldwide. With over three decades of experience in major energy, infrastructure, and rail projects worldwide, Fiona has represented a diverse range of clients, including governments, sponsors, and lenders (commercial and ECAs)...

Apart from her legal expertise, Fiona also possesses extensive professional service firm management experience, having been a member of the governance and management boards for ten years while maintaining a full-time practice. As a former global co-head of diversity and inclusion for six years, she played a pivotal role in sponsoring the development of the firm's Women's and BAME Leadership Programmes. Fiona is known for her logical and decisive thinking, with key strengths in risk identification, management, mitigation, relationship building, team management, and consensus building. Her professional mandates involving governments (both in the UK and overseas) have given her political solid and policy awareness, with a demonstrated track record in setting and delivering strategy.

With regards to mergers and acquisitions, Fiona has advised on numerous high-profile transactions, including Kinetic and Globalvia's recommended offer for Go Ahead, a UK-listed company with train and bus operating businesses in several countries, and Borealis and OTTP's acquisition of HS1, which owns the Channel Tunnel Rail Link.

Fiona has held various leadership positions at Linklaters, including International Board Member (2004-2010), Head of Client Sectors and Executive Committee Member (2010-2014), and Global Head of Diversity and Inclusion (2012-2018). In these roles, she was responsible for formulating and driving the firm's strategy on clients and sectors, assessing partner performance, monitoring and reporting on progress, and overseeing overall management and coordination. Fiona's outstanding contributions to the legal profession have earned her numerous accolades, including recognition as a leading lawyer by Who's Who Legal, Legal 500, and Chambers. She was also awarded the Euromoney Europe Woman in Business Law award as Best in Energy and Natural Resources in 2011 and recognised as one of IFLR's 100 Women Leaders for over 20 years.

In addition to her legal work, Fiona is an active member of various pro bono organisations. She has served as a School Governor at Samuel Rhodes SEN School in Islington, a Lay Member of the Lord Chancellor's Advisory Committee for Magistrates' appointment, and a Conduct Committee member. Fiona enjoys gardening, keeping fit, watching rugby, and travelling in her spare time.

Dr. Gwenllian A Hughes

Industry Expert

Dr. Hughes is an accomplished healthcare professional who has faced and overcome significant personal challenges. Having undergone numerous major operations due to disability, she spent 11 years in a wheelchair as one operation went awry. Dr. Hughes has been both a carer and cared for, gaining unique insights into the needs and experiences of patients and caregivers...

Undaunted by her setbacks, Dr. Hughes regained her status as a Registered Nurse and led the successful turnaround of multiple homes as a Registered Manager. Along the way, she earned both Master's and Doctorate Degrees in social care and management, positioning herself as an expert.

Dr. Hughes is a sought-after speaker who provides inspirational talks on overcoming adversity, motivation, inspiration, leadership, care, compliance, safeguarding, protection of liberty, and the Mental Capacity Act. She also provides consultancy support as a Registered Manager, Responsible Individual, and Health & Safety advisor. As an interim Director or Manager, Dr. Hughes brings high-level leadership and dynamic executive insights that rapidly improve people, performance, and profits. Her favorite assignment is the turnaround of a home, where she can deliver visible and pragmatic leadership underpinned by training, mentoring, and coaching to ensure lasting results.

Dr. Hughes has a wealth of experience working within the regulations of Care in Wales and the Quality Care Commission in England. She is well-versed in meeting and exceeding national standards of care and has been responsible for doing so.

Dr. Hughes has worked closely with a previous UK Prime Minister and was awarded by Her late Majesty Queen Elizabeth for her work. She is bilingual in English and Welsh, holds a current DBS with update service, and working with CQC at a senior level.

Dr. Hughes is a passionate advocate for the future of nursing and social care and has been a speaker at the RCN Congress on this topic. Her forte lies in quickly delivering turnaround solutions via visible and pragmatic leadership, ensuring that the highest standards of care are met and exceeded.

Sultana Pasha

Industry Expert

Sultana possesses an expansive background in regulatory compliance and quality standards, underscored by extensive experience within the realms of Health and Social Care Regulations. Her professional journey includes notable positions such as a Care Quality Commission (CQC) inspector and a Director of Quality and Compliance, roles which have fortified her comprehensive understanding of both public and private sectors of Health and Social Care...

Sultana's cross-sectoral experience has afforded her an in-depth comprehension of the intricacies and nuances of social care. Having witnessed the full spectrum of care delivery, from the exemplary to the inadequate, she has become an unyielding advocate for the maintenance of exceptional care standards. Her primary mission lies in ensuring community members receive culturally sensitive care and support that not only meets but exceeds their expectations.

Her area of expertise centres on aiding health and social care organisations in fulfilling statutory requirements stipulated under the Health and Social Care Act and the standards set by accreditation bodies, such as the British Standards Institute and the Care Quality Commission. As an ISO9001 Internal Auditor, she exemplifies competence and proficiency in her role.

Sultana holds significant experience in external representation of organisations and liaison with statutory bodies. Her efforts consistently aim to uplift the reputation of these organisations while fostering robust, positive relationships. She demonstrates remarkable leadership in overseeing teams and managers, fostering collaborative dynamics with local authorities and Commissioning Groups to ensure optimal performance.

Educationally, Sultana earned a BA Honours degree in Business, and is currently advancing her academic pursuits with an MBA in Senior Leadership. Outside the professional sphere, she is a devoted mother to her two sons, whose accomplishments fill her with immense pride.
